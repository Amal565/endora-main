ABOUT

Venture for Innovation Business and Entrepreneurship (VIBE) is a conglomerate of IIC, IEDC and TBI for upliftment of budding entrepreneurs. The various cells work with aim of providing avenues for creative students to learn, collaborate and transform their innovative ideas into prototypes of viable products and services. The motive of this network is to promote innovation and foster an ecosystem to build strong family in campus. The vision is to commemorate skills in business planning, training, organising and making oneself eligible for value added jobs and services.

01.IEDC
Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Centre (IEDC) is a flagship initiative of Kerala Startup Mission to promote innovation and entrepreneurship among the student and academic fraternity in the educational institutions in the State of Kerala and considered as an umbrella programme that would play an instrumental role in fostering innovation culture in Academic institutions.
02.IIC
The Institutions Innovation Council (IIC) is a coherence approach to align the innovation and entrepreneurship promotion and support. Programs are organized by various departments and ensure year- round activities on campus to promote engagement, learning, and practice of innovation and entrepreneurship among students and faculty. Innovation Council plays a critical role in building and streamlining the innovation and startup ecosystem at the institutional level. The operational model of the IIC has an inbuilt incentive system to measure, evaluate, promote, and reward the best-performing IICs and the innovations that emerge from the IICs to foster healthy competition among institutions and build a strong and vibrant innovation and startup ecosystem on campus.
03.TBI
The need for instruments such as Technology Business Incubator(TBI) has been recognised by the world over for initiating technology led and knowledge driven enterprises. Studies also show that such mechanisms help not only in the growth of technology based new enterprises but also in improving their survival rate substantially.TBIs also facilitate speedy commercialisation of research outputs. Besides providing a host of services to new enterprises TBIs also facilitate an atmosphere congenial for their survival and growth. The TBIs usually provides market survey and marketing assistance, business planning and training, organising management and technical assistance and assistance in obtaining statutory approval.
