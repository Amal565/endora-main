VIBE's IEDC fest(ENDORA) is a platform for budding entrepreneurs to showcase and sell their products. The event includes a job fair, a startup hub, tech-based stalls, entrepreneur talks, workshops, influencers meet and more. It also features an all-Kerala IEDC meet and a ramp walk for differently-abled individuals. Technical competitions and cultural events will also be held.
