LATEX COAGULATOR

KITCHEN GARDENING

By integrating both hydroponics and IOT together, an efficient kitchen gardening system was developed by the students of GEC Kozhikode. Work space and systems with network connections for the development of prototype.

EDU-REVO

The EDU REVO is also known as the Educational Revolution (the revolutionary phase of education), and it aims to address issues in the current education system, such as the use of outdated information and a failure to focus on the true purpose of education, which is to develop one's character and skills for life. Instead, it focuses on converting students become subject matter specialists who are prepared to pass tests, rather than emphasising the practical application of knowledge and the development of cross-disciplinary abilities.

SANITOI

A team of five students developed SANITOI, an automated self-sanitizing toilet module for public restrooms, which received funding from M/s Geojit and was shortlisted in various competitions, including CUSAT - Centre for Advanced Technologies in Disaster Management, Eureka B Plan competition of IIT Bombay, Kerala State Level Pitching of Empowering Youth Hackathon, Swadeshi Microprocessor Challenge, Government of India, and Tech and Innovation Fair, Shaastra 21, IIT Madras.
